The Wolverine

The story begins with Logan (Wolverine) living in self-imposed exile after the events of "X-Men: The Last Stand." Haunted by the memories of Jean Grey's death, he has become a recluse, seeking solace and solitude in the wilderness. However, his peaceful existence is disrupted when he encounters Yukio, a mutant with the ability to foresee death. Yukio, on behalf of her employer Yashida, a wealthy businessman from Japan whom Logan once saved during World War II, persuades Logan to accompany her to Japan.
